BMW M4

The 2023 BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance sports car with a 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder gasoline engine, delivering 503 horsepower. It features an 8-speed automatic transmission and offers an optional xDrive 4-wheel drive system. This car accelerates from 0-62 mph in just 3.9 seconds and can reach a top speed of 180 mph. The engine produces peak torque of 479 lb-ft at 2750 rpm, ensuring a responsive and powerful driving experience.
