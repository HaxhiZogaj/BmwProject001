BMW Z4

The 2023 BMW Z4 epitomizes the modern roadster, blending athletic performance with luxurious open-air driving. This latest model continues to feature BMW's signature design elements, including a sleek and aerodynamic body, a long hood, and short overhangs, all contributing to its dynamic appearance. The Z4 is powered by a choice of potent engines - a turbocharged four-cylinder in the sDrive30i and a more powerful six-cylinder in the M40i model, both delivering exhilarating performance and responsive handling.
