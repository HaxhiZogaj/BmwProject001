BMW i8

The BMW i8 is a revolutionary plug-in hybrid sports car that showcases BMW's commitment to innovation and sustainability. It features a futuristic design with scissor doors, a streamlined profile, and aerodynamic elements. The powertrain combines a turbocharged 3-cylinder gasoline engine with an electric motor, delivering impressive performance while maintaining efficiency. The i8 boasts a lightweight carbon-fiber-reinforced plastic body, contributing to its agility and efficiency.
