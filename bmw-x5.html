BMW X5

The 2023 BMW X5 is a luxury midsize SUV that combines versatility, performance, and refined comfort. It's available with a range of engines, from a turbocharged inline-6 to a more potent V8, and even offers a plug-in hybrid variant, all featuring BMW's TwinPower Turbo technology. The X5 provides an engaging driving experience typical of BMW, along with the practicality of an SUV. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and BMW's intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system. The interior is spacious and luxuriously appointed, featuring state-of-the-art infotainment and advanced driver assistance systems.
