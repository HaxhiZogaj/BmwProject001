BMW 3 Series

The 2023 BMW 3 Series is a blend of comfort and sportiness, appreciated for its corner-carving sports sedan capabilities. It's powered by a premium unleaded fuel engine. The range starts with the 330i, which offers 255 horsepower at 5,000 rpm and 295 lb-ft of torque at 1,550 rpm, featuring a double overhead cam (DOHC) design. This model accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 5.6 seconds. At the higher end, there's the M340i xDrive, boasting 382 horsepower and achieving 0-60 mph in just 4.1 seconds.
